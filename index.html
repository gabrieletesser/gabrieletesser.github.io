Unique Approach to Employee Training

Coach is a unique learning platform that turns employee training into a daily activity through short lessons and interactive learning games. With just 5-10 minutes a day, Coach enables employees to build skills, reinforce knowledge, and drive continuous growth.

Customizable Learning Content

Your training should be as unique as your business. Whether it's sales, product knowledge, customer service techniques, or internal processes, Coach turns your internal content into interactive, role-specific training that's practical and directly relevant. Each lesson aligns with your company's vision and standards, providing employees with practical, role-specific skills to fuel growth and performance.

Save Time on Training Creation

Launching a training or updating an old one often requires a lot of time. With Coach, you just upload your training documents, and Coach transforms them into interactive microlessons and quizzes, reducing training prep time by up to 90%.

Tailored Learning Paths for Every Role

Coach keeps training relevant and efficient by automating learning paths tailored to each role or skill level. Employees receive training at the right time, automatically progressing as they reach milestones, eliminating the need for manual tracking and ensuring continuous development aligned with company goals.

Adaptive, Targeted Learning

As employees learn through the interactive games, Coach identifies their knowledge gaps and recommends the right lessons to help employees improve where it’s most needed. This adaptive approach keeps employees engaged and ensures they continuously build the skills required to perform at their best.

Real-Time Training Insights

Coach gives you a clear view of each employee’s and department’s skills in real-time. Quickly spot knowledge gaps, track progress, and fine-tune training to keep teams aligned with company goals and ready for what’s next.
